About TUBI-AN

For over a decade, TUBI-AN has been the trusted name in water purification and refilling services. We combine cutting-edge technology with unwavering commitment to quality, ensuring every drop of water meets the highest standards of purity and safety.

Our mission is simple: to provide clean, safe, and affordable drinking water to every household in our community. We believe that access to pure water is not just a necessity, but a fundamental right.

Our Facilities

State-of-the-art equipment and processes ensure the highest quality water for your family

Advanced Filtration

Multi-stage filtration system including sediment filters, carbon filters, and reverse osmosis technology to remove all impurities and contaminants.

UV Sterilization

Ultraviolet light sterilization kills 99.9% of bacteria, viruses, and other harmful microorganisms without adding chemicals.

Ozone Treatment

Ozone disinfection provides an additional layer of protection, ensuring complete elimination of pathogens and improving taste.

Quality Testing

Regular laboratory testing and quality control measures ensure consistent water quality that exceeds industry standards.

Eco-Friendly

Sustainable practices with minimal environmental impact, including bottle sanitization and recycling programs.

Maintenance

Regular equipment maintenance and upgrades ensure optimal performance and reliability of our purification systems.
